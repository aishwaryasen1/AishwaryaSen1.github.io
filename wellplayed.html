Video Analysis of Cricket Training for Bowlers

Team Size : 1

Project Guide : Mr. Ketan Kaore

Project Duration : April 2021 - July 2021

Motivation

Having applied pose estimation in a previous project, I realised its potential and wide applicability in the solving of several diverse real-world problems. Thus, this project serves as my second venture into the applications of pose estimation.

Summary

In this project, the analysis of cricket training videos was performed - particularly bowler detetion and pose analysis. Using OpenPose and pre-defined thresholds, a variety of analysis was performed including detection of frame and elevation of jump, instant of release of ball and angle of arm rotation of spinners before release of ball.
